## Findings by Severity

### Critical Severity (1)

#### CRIT-01: Missing Condition Escalation Logic
*   **Status:** Won't Fix
*   **Severity:** Critical
*   **Location:** `src/systems/health.ts`
*   **Description:** The `ActiveCondition.daysUntilCritical` field exists but is intentionally not decremented, preventing health conditions from escalating to critical states and applying permanent debuffs. This affects game balance by simplifying health mechanics.
*   **Rationale:** This is an intentional design decision to simplify gameplay for the initial release, as confirmed in design documentation.
